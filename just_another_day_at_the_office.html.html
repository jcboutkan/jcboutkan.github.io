Just another day at the office

Older >

I don't consider my job boring at all. There are a lot of challenges a (front-end) developer has to conquer on a daily basis. But there is a race going on at this very moment: The Volvo Ocean Race. This race wouldn't have caught my attention if one of my friends didn't mention it, and it caught me.

On october 2nd 2014, 7 boats started the race in Alacante, Spain. On June 27th 2015, after traveling 38739 nautical mile (71744 km), the last in-port race will take place in the port of Gothenburg, Sweden. At this moment they are racing the 4th leg, from Sanya, China, to Auckland, New Zealand.

Now you might be asking yourself what is so exciting about it. Well, it's about 8 men (or 11 women) on a boat, surrendered to nature. They have a goal and have to use the wind, the currents, their intuition and their team spirit to get there first.

Watching the race makes one wonder what forces you are exposed to. Forces that push you forward and forces that hold you back. But where does it push you to or holding you back from? What is your goal? What are the tools you have inside to get to this goal?

The race makes me realize the unlimited potential that lies within me, that we have to challenge ourselfs to dream big, dare greatly and shine brightly. All the experience and training we had brought us to this moment. We have to make the best of it, go 5 steps furter then the rest. That is my race, and it is starting today!
